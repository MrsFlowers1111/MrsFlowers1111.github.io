How to Make Honey Muffins
  1. Mix flour, sugar, and baking powder in a small bowl.
  2. Mix eggs, honey, oak milk and vanilla in a seperate large bowl
  3. Add the dry mixture to the liquid one into batches, mixing thoroughly
  4. Distribute the mixture into greased muffin tins
  5. Bake for 16 mintues, or until lightly browned
  6. Enjoy!
